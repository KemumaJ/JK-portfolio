About Me

Welcome to my webpage! I hope you will enjoy reading and get to learn something about me. My background is in sales and marketing. I have been in the sales environment for the better part of my work life. I enjoy interacting with customers because I believe it is easier to retain happy customers than to acquire one. In an effort to upskill myself, I decided to delve into the programming world. This is something I had never given thought to while at school. My initial desire was to become a journalist. However, due to my dynamic way of thinking I shelved that thought and decided to pursue marketing. I am also a trained digital marketing specialist. A few years back I tried my hand at blogging.
